## HIGH PRIORITY - Add These First

### 1. Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) for Animals ⭐
**Priority**: HIGH
**Category**: Emergency/Neurology
**Purpose**: Assess consciousness level in trauma/neurology patients
**Features**:
- Motor response (1-6)
- Brainstem reflexes (1-5) 
- Level of consciousness (1-5)
- Auto-calculate total score
- Interpretation guide (mild/moderate/severe)
- Species-specific (dog/cat differences)

### 2. Modified Glasgow Pain Scale ⭐
**Priority**: HIGH
**Category**: Pain Management
**Purpose**: Objective pain assessment
**Features**:
- Posture/activity scoring
- Vocalization
- Attention to wound
- Response to touch
- Overall demeanor
- Species-specific versions

### 3. Quality of Life Calculator (HHHHHMM Scale) ⭐
**Priority**: HIGH
**Category**: Hospice/Palliative Care
**Purpose**: End-of-life decision making
**Features**:
- Hurt, Hunger, Hydration, Hygiene, Happiness, Mobility, More good days
- Score interpretation
- Recommendations

### 4. Blood Pressure Categorization (ACVIM Guidelines) ⭐
**Priority**: HIGH
**Category**: Cardiology
**Purpose**: Classify BP readings
**Features**:
- Systolic/Diastolic input
- Risk category output
- Treatment recommendations
- Target organ damage assessment

### 5. Apgar Score for Neonates ⭐
**Priority**: HIGH
**Category**: Emergency/Reproduction
**Purpose**: Assess newborn viability
**Features**:
- Heart rate, Respiration, Muscle tone, Reflexes, Color
- 1-minute and 5-minute scoring
- Intervention guide
