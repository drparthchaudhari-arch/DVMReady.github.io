How to Use This Library

  1. Species-first pass: review core conditions by species.
  2. Systems pass: cross-link cardio, respiratory, GI, neuro, urinary, and reproductive patterns.
  3. High-yield drill: revisit emergencies, toxicities, fluid and electrolyte protocols.
  4. Ethics and public health pass: VCPR, reportables, and zoonoses.
  5. Active recall: use checklists at the end for timed self-testing.

NAVLE Blueprint Alignment

  • Small Animal (Canine/Feline): approximately 40%
  • Large Animal (Equine/Bovine/Small Ruminant/Porcine): approximately 40%
  • Avian/Exotic/Aquatic: approximately 10%
  • Public Health and Practice Management: approximately 10%

Species-First Core Topics

Bovine
  • Cardio: atrial fibrillation, CHF causes, bovine high mountain disease, TEME.
  • GI: LDA/RDA, abomasal volvulus, hardware disease, vagal indigestion, calf enteritis, cecal disorders.
  • Neuro: listeriosis, PEM, tetanus, hypomagnesemic tetany, rabies rule-outs.
  • Respiratory: BRDC/shipping fever, IBR, calf pneumonia, vena caval thrombosis.
  • Repro: dystocia, uterine torsion, retained placenta, milk fever, abortion workup, mastitis control.
  • Multisystemic: BVD, clostridials, ketosis, leptospirosis, plant toxicities, anthrax, brucellosis.
Equine
  • GI/colic triage: surgical indicators, reflux interpretation, impaction vs strangulating lesions.
  • MSK: laminitis, navicular disease, tendon/ligament injury, septic arthritis, developmental orthopedic disease.
  • Neuro: EPM, EHV-1 myeloencephalopathy, WNV, EEE/WEE, tetanus, botulism.
  • Respiratory: pleuropneumonia, heaves/RAO, EIPH, laryngeal hemiplegia, guttural pouch disease.
  • Repro/neonate: retained placenta (greater than 3h), dystocia response, NI, FPT, septic foal.
  • Endocrine/metabolic: PPID, EMS, hyperlipemia.
Canine
  • Cardio: DMVD, DCM, CHF stabilization, arrhythmias, heartworm disease, pericardial effusion.
  • Endocrine: DM/DKA, Addison's, Cushing's, hypothyroidism.
  • GI: parvo, pancreatitis, GDV, foreign body, IBD, EPI, PSS.
  • Heme/immune: IMHA, ITP, coagulopathies, lymphoma, hemangiosarcoma, mast cell tumor.
  • Neuro: IVDD, vestibular disease, myasthenia gravis, seizures, meningitis/GME.
  • Respiratory/MSK/skin: CIRDC, pneumonia, tracheal collapse, CCL rupture, osteosarcoma, atopy, pyoderma, demodicosis.
Feline
  • CKD and FLUTD/urethral obstruction emergency pathway.
  • Hyperthyroidism, diabetes mellitus, pancreatitis/cholangitis/IBD triad.
  • HCM and arterial thromboembolism pattern recognition.
  • Upper respiratory disease, asthma, stomatitis, lymphoma, FeLV/FIV/FIP differentials.
Small Ruminants
  • Haemonchus anemia and FAMACHA-based control.
  • Enterotoxemia, pregnancy toxemia, coccidiosis, listeriosis, PEM.
  • CLA, CAE/OPP, urolithiasis, abortion panel (Chlamydia/Campylobacter/Toxo).
Porcine
  • PRRS, PCV2, mycoplasma pneumonia, APP, swine influenza.
  • PED/TGE/colibacillosis, Lawsonia, swine dysentery.
  • Erysipelas, Strep suis meningitis, edema disease, MMA syndrome.
  • Foreign animal disease awareness: ASF, CSF.
Poultry and Aquatics
  • Poultry: Newcastle, avian influenza, Marek's, coccidiosis, infectious bronchitis/laryngotracheitis.
  • Aquatics: ich, columnaris, gill parasites, ammonia/nitrite toxicity, fish mycobacteriosis.

Systems-Based Integration

  • Cardiovascular: congenital vs acquired disease, arrhythmia treatment logic, shock subtype differentiation.
  • Respiratory: upper vs lower airway patterns, pleural disease, species-specific syndromes.
  • Gastrointestinal: obstruction vs inflammation vs metabolic causes; colic decision thresholds.
  • Urinary: AKI/CKD staging, urolith patterns, postrenal emergencies, electrolyte consequences.
  • Reproductive: dystocia algorithms, postpartum emergencies, abortion investigations by species.
  • Nervous: seizure localization, spinal lesion logic, toxic vs infectious vs inflammatory causes.
  • Musculoskeletal: developmental vs degenerative vs traumatic vs immune-mediated lameness.
  • Integumentary: parasite/fungal/bacterial/allergic/neoplastic pattern separation.

High-Yield Emergency Topics

  • Shock triage and fluid bolus strategy by species.
  • CPCR protocol fundamentals and post-resuscitation priorities.
  • DKA stabilization sequence (fluids, insulin, potassium/phosphorus, trigger control).
  • GDV stabilization and surgery timing.
  • Urethral obstruction in cats and hyperkalemia management.
  • Milk fever/hypocalcemia treatment sequence in cattle.
  • Toxin response frameworks: rodenticides, ethylene glycol, xylitol, lilies, permethrin, organophosphates.
  • Anesthesia basics: ASA stratification, monitoring, hypotension/hypoventilation correction.

Ethics, Law, and Public Health

  • VCPR requirements, informed consent, record quality, controlled-substance handling.
  • Reportable disease responsibilities and jurisdictional communication pathways.
  • Food-animal ELDU restrictions, withdrawal times, and antimicrobial stewardship.
  • Zoonotic priorities: rabies, leptospirosis, brucellosis, toxoplasmosis, salmonellosis, ringworm.
  • One Health principles linking animal, human, and environmental outcomes.

Active Recall Checklist

  • Can you list the top emergency interventions for DKA, GDV, urethral obstruction, and shock?
  • Can you separate Addison's vs Cushing's and Type I vs Type II IVDD under time pressure?
  • Can you identify species-specific reportable diseases and first response steps?
  • Can you outline five common causes of abortion for cattle, horses, and small ruminants?
  • Can you work up PU/PD, chronic diarrhea, colic, and abortion storm using a structured algorithm?
