Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use DVMReady for free?

Yes! All clinical calculators, reference materials, and basic NAVLE practice questions are completely free. No credit card required.

What happens to my data if I cancel Premium?

You'll keep all your data, but sync features will pause. You can still access everything locally and export your data anytime.

Is Premium required for clinical use?

No. All clinical calculators and reference tools remain free. Premium only adds advanced study features for NAVLE preparation.
