## CONCLUSION

**DVMReady is the BETTER choice for:**
- ✅ Veterinary students
- ✅ Those needing exotic animal dosing
- ✅ Budget-conscious users
- ✅ Those wanting study resources
- ✅ Multi-device users

**DVMCalc is BETTER for:**
- Established vets who prefer apps
- Those wanting maximum calculators
- iOS-only users
- Those willing to pay for native experience

**Bottom Line**: DVMReady offers 90% of DVMCalc's functionality for FREE, plus exotic species and study tools. For students and new grads, DVMReady is the clear winner.
