Common Interaction Types

Contraindicated / Severe

Avoid combination or use extreme caution. Significant risk of adverse effects.

Moderate Interaction

May require dose adjustment, monitoring, or timing separation. Assess risk-benefit.

Mild Interaction

Generally manageable. Monitor for decreased efficacy or mild side effects.
