üìö About Metabolic Weight

Metabolic weight (BW^0.75) accounts for the non-linear relationship between body size and metabolic rate. Kleiber's law states that metabolic rate scales to the 3/4 power of body mass.

  • Linear (mg/kg): Simple but overestimates for large animals
  • Metabolic (BW^0.75): Better for drugs eliminated by metabolism
  • BSA (mg/m¬≤): Best for chemo and cell-cycle specific drugs

Formula: Metabolic Weight = Body Weight(kg)^0.75
