üìä Pain Score Interpretation

0 = No Pain

No analgesia required. Continue monitoring.

1-3 = Mild Pain

Consider mild analgesia (NSAID). Reassess in 2-4 hours.

4-6 = Moderate Pain

Analgesia required (opioid ¬± NSAID). Reassess in 1-2 hours.

7-10 = Severe Pain

Immediate analgesia required (opioid + multimodal). Reassess in 30-60 min.

Reference: Modified Glasgow Composite Measure Pain Scale. Based on Holton et al. (1998) for dogs and Calvo et al. (2014) for cats. Score range 0-10.
